## Top 5 Focus Hacks Backed by Neuroscience

One of the best focus hacks for deep work in 2026 | Neurosity is time blocking. This means setting specific times for deep work and sticking to them. It helps your brain know when to switch into focus mode.

Another hack is the Pomodoro Technique. This involves working for 25 minutes and taking a 5-minute break. It keeps your brain from getting overwhelmed and maintains your focus over longer periods.

Reducing multitasking is another key hack. Your brain can only handle one task at a time, and switching between tasks uses up mental energy. By focusing on one thing, you can get more done in less time.

Mindfulness and meditation can also help. These practices train your brain to stay focused and reduce distractions. Even just 10 minutes a day can make a difference.
