## Introduction to Passive Income in 2026

Passive income is no longer a dream for most people. In 2026, it’s becoming a realistic way to earn money with minimal effort. More people are finding creative ways to build streams of income that work even when they’re not actively working.

## Affiliate Marketing and Online Courses

Affiliate marketing is one of the easiest ways to start earning passively. You promote products and earn a commission on every sale. It’s perfect for people who enjoy sharing their knowledge or interests online.

Online courses are another powerful tool. If you have expertise in a subject, you can create and sell courses on platforms like Udemy or Teachable. These platforms take care of the logistics, so you just need to focus on content.

The "20 Passive Income Ideas That Actually Work in 2026 | Agiled" guide covers both affiliate marketing and online courses in detail. It gives you real examples and tips on how to get started.

## Real Estate Investing and Dividend Stocks

Real estate investing is a traditional but effective way to build passive income. You can invest in rental properties or real estate investment trusts (REITs). These options provide regular income through rent or dividends.

Dividend stocks are another solid option. They offer regular income without the need to manage physical properties. Companies like Coca-Cola or Procter & Gamble are known for their consistent dividends.

The "20 Passive Income Ideas That Actually Work in 2026 | Agiled" guide explains how to choose the right stocks and real estate opportunities. It also highlights the importance of long-term planning.

## Creating Digital Products and Licensing

Creating digital products is a great way to earn money with little ongoing work. You can design templates, write e-books, or create software that people can use repeatedly. These products can be sold on platforms like Etsy or Gumroad.

Licensing your work is another way to earn passive income. If you create something unique, like a logo or a brand identity, you can license it to others for a fee. This can be a steady source of income over time.

The "20 Passive Income Ideas That Actually Work in 2026 | Agiled" guide provides step-by-step instructions on how to create and market digital products. It also includes tips on finding the right audience for your work.

## Automated Businesses and Subscription Models

Automated businesses can generate income with little daily involvement. You can use tools like Shopify or WordPress to set up an e-commerce store that runs itself. These tools handle customer service, inventory, and payments.

Subscription models are becoming more popular. People are willing to pay for regular access to content, products, or services. You can offer a monthly subscription for exclusive content or a service that delivers value regularly.
