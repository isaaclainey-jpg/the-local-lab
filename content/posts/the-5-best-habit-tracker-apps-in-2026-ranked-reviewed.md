Next up is Streaks. This app is perfect for those who like to track their habits in a clean and simple interface. It offers calendar views, progress tracking, and the ability to set reminders. The 5 Best Habit Tracker Apps in 2026 all aim to make tracking easier, but Streaks stands out for its minimalist design.

HabitBull is another app that makes habit tracking easy. It allows users to set daily goals and track their progress in real time. The app also sends notifications to help users stay on track. It’s ideal for people who need a gentle nudge to keep going.

Then there’s Loop, which uses a unique approach by focusing on habit stacking. It helps users build new habits by linking them to existing ones. This method is based on psychological principles and has been shown to improve habit formation.

Finally, there’s MyHabit, which is great for people who want a highly customizable experience. It allows users to set up their own habit categories, set reminders, and track progress over time. The 5 Best Habit Tracker Apps in 2026 all offer something different, so it’s important to choose the one that fits your lifestyle best.
